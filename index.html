Description of the Problem

  • Time delays in reporting.
  • Errors in interpretation due to fatigue or high workload.
  • Limited access to expert radiologists in remote areas.

These issues highlight the need for an automated AI-based diagnostic solution that can provide accurate, fast, and accessible results.

Solution Overview

The major objectives of RADISIST are:

  • Analyze CT scan images using AI.
  • Accurately detect and classify multiple diseases with deep learning.
  • Generate detailed diagnostic reports for patients and clinicians.
  • Reduce dependency on manual interpretation and improve efficiency.
  • Provide a user-friendly web interface for easy accessibility.

Expected Results

  • Real-time CT scan analysis with highlighted abnormalities and medical insights.
  • Improved efficiency and accuracy in diagnostic decision-making.
  • Reliable, accessible tool for regions with limited radiology expertise.
